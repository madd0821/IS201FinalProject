Personal

Beyond the Resume

I’ve always been drawn to experiences that bring people together—whether it’s a late-night inner-tube water polo match, a competitive intramural basketball game, or simply spending time outdoors with friends and family. I find the greatest joy in moments where people connect, laugh, and create lasting memories. That’s why I’m so excited about the Experience and Design Management program at BYU. My passion for creating meaningful, memorable experiences in both casual and structured settings aligns perfectly with the program’s focus on human-centered design and leadership. I want to learn how to turn those everyday joyful moments into intentional, impactful experiences that can shape organizations, communities, and individual lives for the better.

Madeline Watkins 2025
